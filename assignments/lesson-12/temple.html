Denver Colorado Temple

Along with our three nights of rodeo, we have parades and a great downtown sidewalk sale. The sidewalk sale brings vendors from our local vicinity and from surrounding states. Everything from jewelry to camping gear to clothes, gifts, signs, decorating items…you name it, you can find it, along with all kinds of great food booths. Delicious foods, drinks, sweets can be found as you stroll down Preston’s main street and Oneida street.
