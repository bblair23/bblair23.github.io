Dog Sled Races in Soda Springs

According to the Idaho State Journal, "The Rocky Mountain Sled Dog Championship will be held Jan. 25 and 26 in Soda Springs. The races are free to the public." They also stated, " Registration and a social for the event will be Friday evening, Jan. 24, at 7 p.m. at the National Oregon/California Trail Center in Montpelier (847-3800). The location for the actual races on Jan. 25 and 26 is listed as U.S. Highway 30 at Sulphur Canyon Road."
